About Us

Zimbabwe's Dynamic Construction & Architectural Firm

Anajoe Logos Investments (Pvt) Ltd is a dynamic construction and architectural firm registered in Zimbabwe and committed to building lasting structures — and lasting relationships.

Incorporated
31 May 2024

Founded and incorporated on 31 May 2024 under the Companies and Other Business Entities Act [Chapter 24:31], we are fully licensed and registered with relevant authorities.

Our team blends youthful energy with seasoned expertise, providing superior construction services to both public and private sector clients nationwide. We efficiently meet the diverse construction needs of our clients across Zimbabwe — delivering projects from foundations to finishes and renovations.

AJ Logos is owned and operated by a multidisciplinary team of Construction Engineers, Civil Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Quantity Surveyors, Land Surveyors, and skilled Artisans. This depth of in-house expertise allows us to handle every phase of a construction project under one roof — giving our clients a seamless, single-point experience from the initial plan drawing through to the final coat of paint.

What We Do

Our Construction Services

End-to-end construction delivered entirely in-house. From the first architectural sketch to the final coat of paint — AJ Logos handles every phase with precision and professionalism.

Design & Planning

Architectural drawings (3D & 2D), detailed plan preparation and full council approval processes. We ensure your vision is technically sound before a single brick is laid.

Construction & Building

Full residential and commercial building works. From foundations and brickwork to structural framing — we manage every phase with precision, delivering on time and within budget.

Roofing & Structural Works

Professional roofing installation, plastering and skimming finishes. Our structural team ensures every roof is built for durability and performance against Zimbabwe's climate.

Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing

Plumbing, drainlaying, electrical installations and mechanical systems. Certified in-house teams handle all MEP works to code, ensuring safety and compliance throughout.

Finishes & Renovations

Tiling, painting, decorating, wall cladding and maintenance works. We transform spaces with quality finishes — including premium wall cladding that elevates any exterior aesthetic.

Quantity Surveying & BOQ

Accurate bill of quantities and cost planning before construction begins. No hidden costs, no surprises — just transparent, precise budgeting you can trust from day one.
